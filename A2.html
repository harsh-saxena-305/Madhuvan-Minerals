About Us

Madhuvan Minerals is a commercial mines. Which has been started in month of january in 2019 after a very long process to get the mines accreditated by the government of india. This mines produces different types of minerals which are Pyrophillite, Granite ,Dolomite and Diaspore and sell these minerals in solid and powder form.
